Instant Pot Quinoa Burrito Bowls

Description

Quick, simple and delicious quinoa burrito bowls! This recipe is great to make and store for a quick meal after work or to pack on the go for a healthy lunch. Eat as is, or wrap in a tortilla with your favorite meat and burrito toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 can Black beans
  • 1/2 cup Salsa
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Green pepper
  • 2 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Saute onion and green pepper in Instant Pot until onion is translucent (about 8 minutes). Add cumin and salt about halfway through
  2. Turn off the Instant Pot for a moment. Add the water and quinoa, stir.
  3. Add drained beans and salsa. DO NOT STIR
  4. Cook on "Rice" setting for 12 minutes
  5. Allow pressure to naturally release for 15 minutes
  6. Release pressure, stir and serve or store!
